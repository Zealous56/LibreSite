Author info:
My name is Nicholas Johnson. My favorite subjects besides computers are philosophy, mathematics, biology, physics, spirituality, medicine, history, languages, culture and lots more. I have been fascinated with computers since the age of about 7. I immediately took to them. At age 13 I taught myself about circuits and hardware such as registers, ALU's, serial and parallel buses, memory and logic gates. At age 18 I began studying programming formally in college.

Site info:
This site is for sharing my projects and beliefs on computing. Within computing, I am especially focused on libre software, decentralized systems, ethical computing, privacy, security, and anonymity. I may occasionally talk about other things.
